This is the story...

In the quiet town of Eldridge, the old clock tower struck midnight, its chimes echoing through the misty streets. Sarah, returning home from a late shift, felt a chill brush past her, though she was alone. As she hurried her pace, she heard soft whispers weaving through the fog, calling her name. The shadows danced around her, and she glimpsed a figure just out of reach, its eyes glowing like embers. By dawn, the townsfolk found her abandoned phone on the pavement, but Sarah had vanished without a trace.
